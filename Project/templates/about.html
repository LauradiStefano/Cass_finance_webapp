Project
Web-based Application is a software that can be accessed by users through Internet and that can be allocated in a central server or in a user's local system. The aim of this project, as a result of a growing passion for finance and informatics through the years of my Specialisation Degree in Quantitative Finance and Business Analytics, is to give to students and stakeholders the possibility to put into practice some financial models. Moreover, web-based application gives a straightforwardly access to the necessary financial information anywhere and at any time, together with a common and user-friendly space for the interaction with co-operators or people in general. It is characterized by simplicity of use and implementation, and it can be easily customized. Final objective of our project is to obtain an advanced, from the programming point of view, Web-based Application accessible to Web-users by means of an external cloud server.
Team
